1. Task


Design a Platformer

2. Idea


Let's design a fast paced 3d platformer with elements like wallrunning/walljumping and a dimension shift mechanic inspired from Titanfall 2.

3. Who does what?


While my other 2 group members focused mainly on UI, Art and Programming, my main task was the level design
My plan was to design 3 levels:
  1. Tutorial: A tutorial level where the player gets to know the controls and the different mechanics

  2. Normal: The "normal" level where the player has to show that he understood the mechanics from the tutorial while getting introduced to new feature like the cannon, which can knock you off platforms. Every jump the player has to shift dimensions, giving the game a rhythm like feeling.

  3. Hard: The last level should be a challenging (bonus) level, only playable if you beat the levels before that. The "rhythm" feeling (from the previous levels) is completely abandoned, forcing the player to think before every jump, if he has to switch (dimensions) or not. Furthermore there are upgraded cannons and multiple paths to the end giving the player the chance to find the fastest way to find the finishing line.
