About me
My name is Artjom Zakoyan, I am 20 years old and currently studying Informatic: Game Engineering (B.Sc.) in Munich.

Ever since I was a child, I have been highly curious and have cultivated a broad spectrum of interests. These ranged from history (especially the Middle Ages and pirates) to the wildlife and the cosmos.

Through my uncle I discovered my love and interest for video games and since then my dream was always to make games. Books and movies were also great but couldn't create as much personal immersion as video games.

Because of my Software Engineering course in my high school I sparked interest in programming, so I decided to combine Games and Programming and study Informatic: Game Engineering at the TUM
During my work in university projects and private projects I discovered my passion for designing experiences, whether through Game Design in Video Games or through UI/UX in Apps.

Since the experiences of these two media are contrary to each other (especially the UI) it helped me differentiate what the user/player needs (and wants) for either one.
Furthermore, I believe that the most important thing is when designing experiences is to put yourself into the user/player of your media. Only through a change of perspective you can give the user/player the experience you designed for him.

I aim to craft the type of experiences that have made such a lasting impression on me, and helped shape me into the person I am today.
