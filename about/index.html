About
My name is Artjom Zakoyan, I am 21 years old and currently studying Informatic: Game Engineering (B.Sc.) in Munich.

Ever since I was a child, I have been highly curious and have cultivated a broad spectrum of interests. These ranged from history (especially the Middle Ages and pirates) to the wildlife and the cosmos.

Through my uncle I discovered my love and interest for video games and since then my dream was always to make games. Books and movies were also great but couldn't create as much personal immersion as video games.

Because of my Software Engineering course in my high school I sparked interest in programming, so I decided to combine game development and programming and study Informatic: Game Engineering at the TUM. During my work in university projects and private projects I discovered my passion for designing experiences. Furthermore I experimented and self-taught myself skills in different fields ranging from App Development to UI/UX Design. The latter had also enabled me to build up a sidehustle as a freelancer.

During the development of my 2D Combat Demo I had a lot of fun with combat design and I will probably specialize in this field.

I aim to craft the type of experiences that have made such a lasting impression on me, and helped shape me into the person I am today.
